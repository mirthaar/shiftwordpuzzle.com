Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Shift ("we," "our," or "us") is a daily word puzzle game for iOS. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Information you provide:

If you create an account, we collect your first name and email address, or your name from Apple or Google if you sign in that way.

Information collected automatically:

  • Game data — your puzzle results, guess history, streaks, and win/loss record. Stored on your device and, if signed in, synced to our cloud database (Firebase Firestore).
  • Analytics — anonymized usage data such as puzzles started, guesses submitted, and win/loss events. Provided by Firebase Analytics. On iOS 14+, only collected if you grant App Tracking Transparency permission.
  • Device information — basic info such as device type and iOS version, collected by Firebase.

How We Use Your Information

  • To save your game progress and sync it across devices
  • To calculate and display your stats and streaks
  • To send a daily puzzle reminder notification (only if you opt in)
  • To improve the game through anonymized analytics

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except as described below.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and analytics. firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Sign-In — optional sign-in method. policies.google.com/privacy
  • Sign in with Apple — optional sign-in method. apple.com/legal/privacy

Push Notifications

If you opt in, Shift sends a daily local notification reminding you to play. You can turn this off at any time in the app under Settings, or in your iPhone's notification settings.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the app under Settings → Privacy Settings → Delete My Account. This permanently deletes your account, game history, and all associated data from our servers.

Children's Privacy

Shift is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
